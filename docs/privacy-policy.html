Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 16, 2026

Summary: Military Flash Cards does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All your study data stays on your device.

Introduction

Military Flash Cards ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Do NOT Collect

The App is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Any data that leaves your device

Local Data Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Flashcard decks you create or import
  • Your study progress and review history
  • App preferences and settings

This data is stored using your device's local database and is never transmitted to any external servers.

No Internet Required

The App is designed to work completely offline. No internet connection is required for any functionality, and no data is ever sent over the network.

No Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by:

  • Using the "Reset Database" option in Settings
  • Uninstalling the App from your device

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
