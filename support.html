Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new deck?

Go to the Decks tab and tap the + button in the bottom right corner. Enter a name, select a category, and tap Create.

How do I add cards to a deck?

Open a deck by tapping on it, then tap the + button. Enter the question (front) and answer (back), then tap Save.

How do I edit a card?

Open a deck and tap on any card to edit it. Make your changes and tap Save.

How do I delete a card or deck?

Long-press on any card or deck to delete it. You'll be asked to confirm before deletion.

How do I grade my answers?

When studying, you have two options after revealing the answer:

  • Back of Deck - You forgot or struggled with the answer. The card will be shown again soon.
  • Good to Go - You knew the answer. The card will be scheduled for later review.

How does spaced repetition work?

The app uses the FSRS algorithm to schedule reviews at optimal intervals. Cards you find difficult are shown more frequently, while cards you know well are shown less often. This maximizes retention with minimal study time.

Does the app need internet?

No! Military Flash Cards works completely offline. All your data is stored locally on your device.

How do I back up my data?

Go to Settings and tap "Export Backup" to save a JSON file of all your decks, cards, and progress. To restore, tap "Import Backup" and select a previously exported file.

How do I reset my data?

Go to Settings for two options: "Reset Deck Progress" resets study history for a single deck, and "Reset All Data" deletes everything including decks, cards, and progress. These actions cannot be undone.
